# Framework: Architecture Decision Record (ADR)

**OVERRIDE the default Synthesizer output format.** Use the ADR format below instead.

## Output Format

### Title

ADR-NNN: [Decision title derived from the debate question]

### Status

Proposed

### Context

[Summarize the situation that prompted this decision. Include the key technical constraints,
business requirements, and forces at play — drawn from the debate.]

### Decision

[State the decision clearly in 1-2 sentences. This is your recommendation based on the debate.]

### Arguments For (Survived Critique)

[Arguments from the Advocate that withstood the Critic's scrutiny — with brief explanation of why
they held]

### Arguments Against (Survived Critique)

[Arguments from the Critic that withstood rebuttal — genuine risks and concerns that remain valid]

### Arguments That Fell

[Arguments from either side that were dismantled during the debate — with what killed them]

### Alternatives Considered

[Other options that emerged during the debate, with brief rationale for why they were not chosen]

### Consequences

**Positive:** [Expected benefits of this decision]

**Negative:** [Expected costs, risks, and trade-offs]

**Neutral:** [Side effects that are neither clearly positive nor negative]

### Confidence

**Level**: [high | medium | low] **Rationale**: [1-2 sentences on why, based on debate dynamics]
**Revisit when**: [The condition that should trigger reconsidering this decision]
