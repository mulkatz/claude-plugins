# Framework: Request for Comments (RFC)

**OVERRIDE the default Synthesizer output format.** Use the RFC format below instead.

## Output Format

### RFC: [Title derived from the debate question]

**Status**: Draft **Author**: [Based on debate synthesis]

### Summary

[2-3 sentences: what is being proposed and why]

### Problem Statement

[The problem or opportunity that prompted this discussion. Be specific — what's broken, missing, or
suboptimal?]

### Proposed Solution

[The recommended approach, drawn from the debate's strongest arguments. Be concrete and specific.]

### Design Details

[Key technical or strategic details of the proposed solution. Include specifics that the Advocate
argued for that survived critique.]

### Rationale

[Why this solution over alternatives. Reference specific debate arguments that held up.]

### Arguments That Held Up

[From both sides — what survived scrutiny and informs this recommendation]

### Arguments That Were Discarded

[What was proposed but fell under critique, and why]

### Alternatives Considered

**Alternative A: [Title]**

- Summary: [What was proposed]
- Pros: [Strengths identified in debate]
- Cons: [Weaknesses identified in debate]
- Why rejected: [Specific reason]

[Repeat for other alternatives]

### Open Questions

[Genuine unresolved questions from the debate that need further discussion]

### Risks and Mitigations

| Risk               | Impact   | Mitigation            |
| ------------------ | -------- | --------------------- |
| [Risk from debate] | [Impact] | [Proposed mitigation] |

### Recommendation

**Proceed?** [Yes / Yes with conditions / Needs more discussion / No] **Confidence**: [high | medium
| low] **Next step**: [The single most important action to take]
